About Me

Ryan Twemlow

Hi there! My name is Ryan Twemlow and I am the creator of Quizz, a start-up that uses artificial intelligence to generate quizzes for students and teachers. I created Quizz because I was tired of struggling to find good quizzes or make my own to use for studying. I wanted to create a platform that would allow users to easily create and take quizzes on any topic.
